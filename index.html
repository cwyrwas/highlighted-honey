About Us

Highlighted Honey Salon, located at 1114 Scalp Ave, Johnstown, PA, is new to the area and we invite you to join us at our chic, trendy location! We are a full service salon and offer many services including: color services, nail services, waxing, hair extensions, haircuts, hairstyles and even wedding packages! At Highlighted Honey Salon, we are ready to make you feel beautiful and refreshed. Whether you're looking for a new look or just to enjoy a day of pampering, it will be an experience you won't forget! If you are looking for a new salon or stylist, let us welcome you with a new client offer!

Highlighted Honey is open by appointment every day except Tuesday (closed). Weekly hours are typically from 8:00 am - 8:00 pm and weekend hours vary 9:00 am - 4:00 pm. Hours are subject to change and if possible, we will work with your schedule to accommodate you in any way we can!
